# listen2post

Generates Yoshiori-style blog posts from LISTEN voice diary episodes.

## Usage

Run `/blog` to go from episode selection to finished blog post in one flow.

## Notes

- Always follow the writing style rules in `.claude/skills/blog/SKILL.md`
- Restructure transcript content for readability — don't just transcribe verbatim
- Target: conversational but not raw speech, written but not formal
